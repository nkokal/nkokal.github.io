How Iris Works

Paste texts or messages from your date/partner, and our AI pipeline will tell you exactly who they are and how to talk to them.

✍️

You can use anything they’ve written: texts, insta captions, tweets, comments, letters, emails. No paraphrasing!

🙋‍♀️

Samples where they’re expressing themselves, having a real conversation, or are more emotional will do better.

🙅‍♀️

Samples that use slang or are planning things like meetings or dates, won’t do as well.

💁‍♀️

The more samples you put in, the more accurate the result.
